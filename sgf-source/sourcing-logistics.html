— Farm · Factory · Carrier · Delivery · Market —

Reliable farm-to-market logistics for global buyers.

SGF Sourcing provides end-to-end logistics from Ben Tre to your port. MOQ starts from one container, with efficient shipping, private labeling, and custom branding solutions for international distribution.

— Our Supply Chain —

Five stages, one team.

01

Farm

Direct partnerships with GlobalGAP-certified cooperatives in Ben Tre and Long An. No middlemen, no auction-floor markup.

02

Factory

Our HACCP packing house — wash, peel, shape, wrap, and label. Each batch handled within 24 hours of harvest.

03

Carrier

Refrigerated container service from Cat Lai (HCMC) and Hai Phong. Approved carrier list across Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM, and ONE.

04

Delivery

FOB, CFR, or CIF — your choice. 30–45 day door-to-door sailing windows, with full documentation pack on arrival.

05

Market

Your brand, your shelf, your customer. Private labeling and custom packaging fully supported — the last mile is yours.
