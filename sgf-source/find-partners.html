— R&D Partner Search —

Looking for partners to grow with us.

SGF Sourcing is opening an R&D track for universities, food-science labs, post-harvest innovators, and packaging specialists. If your work touches fresh young coconut — varietal selection, shelf-life extension, sustainable packaging, traceability — we want to talk.

— Who We Are Looking For —

Four kinds of R&D partner.

Universities & Research Institutes

Agronomy, food science, post-harvest biology, supply-chain economics. We host site visits in Ben Tre, share orchard data, and co-author when the work warrants publication.

Packaging & Materials Specialists

Compostable films, low-carbon shrink, smart labels, retail-ready containers. If you can extend our 60-day shelf life or cut plastic from our packing line, we will pilot it.

Food-Tech & Processing Partners

Cold-pressed water, value-added formats, RTD products, by-product valorization (husk, shell, coir). We have the raw material at industrial volume.

Traceability & Digital Partners

QR-based traceability, blockchain provenance, IoT cold-chain monitoring. Help us tell every importer exactly which orchard a container came from.

— What We Bring —

A working farm, a working packing house, a working supply chain.

Field Access

Direct, ongoing access to GlobalGAP-certified coconut orchards in Ben Tre and Long An — at any stage of the growing and harvest cycle.

Industrial Volume

Trial volumes from a single pallet to multi-container production runs. Real-world test data, not laboratory mock-ups.

Export Pipeline

An active export pipeline to EU, North America, the Middle East, and East Asia — your innovation reaches buyers, not just journals.
