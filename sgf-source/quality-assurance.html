— Quality Assurance —

Certified. Tested. Traceable.

SGF Sourcing is committed to maintaining GlobalGAP and HACCP certifications. Every shipment is supported by Eurofins third-party testing to assure food safety, freshness, and international export compliance.

— Our Standards —

Three certifications, one promise.

GlobalGAP

Good Agricultural Practices certification covering our partnered Ben Tre and Long An farm cooperatives. Audited annually, MRL-compliant for the EU, the US, and Japan.

HACCP

Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points — applied across our packing house, cold chain, and documentation flow. Every nut is logged from grove to container.

Eurofins Third-Party Testing

Pre-shipment residue and microbiological testing by Eurofins on every shipment, with results released to the buyer alongside the Bill of Lading.

i.

Support

ii.

Service

Sample handling, branding artwork review, customs document coordination, post-arrival quality follow-up — included, not extra.
