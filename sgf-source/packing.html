— Packing Formats —

Seven ways to ship.

From retail-shelf-ready Diamond Cut to bulk industrial — we tailor every container to how your buyers shop.

i.

Diamond Cut

National-standard export form. Green husk fully removed, diamond-shaped carve, PE shrink-film with branded label.

9 or 12 pcs / carton
ii.

Polished / Bald

Deeper peel than Diamond Cut. Compact form, maximum container efficiency. Shrink film or vacuum-sealed.

Bulk export · vacuum option
iii.

Easy-Open

Diamond Cut or Polished with laser-engraved pull-ring. Includes straw and wooden spoon. Retail and HoReCa ready.

12 pcs / carton
iv.

Fresh Green

Minimal processing. Surface cleaned, stem trimmed. Most natural flavor. PP mesh bags or loose-loaded reefer.

10–12 pcs / mesh bag
v.

Semi-Husked

Outer green skin removed; protective fiber retained. High impact resistance. No film required for transit.

Mesh bags or ventilated cartons
vi.

Tray Pack

Retail and supermarket. One or two peeled coconuts on tray with cling-wrap. Protects diamond tips during handling.

1–2 pcs / tray
vii.

Bulk Industrial

For raw-material processing. Pallet cages or wooden bins. No carton. Highest tonnage per container.

Pallet cages · processors only
