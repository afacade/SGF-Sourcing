Nine ways to ship a Vietnamese coconut.

From the whole, husk-on Fresh Whole Coconut to the brand-ready Laser-engraved Diamond. Each format below lists weight per nut, carton count, and packing detail — pick the shape that matches your channel.

i.

Fresh Whole Coconut

Whole young coconut with the natural green husk preserved. The most natural-looking presentation — ideal for traditional markets and HoReCa channels that value authenticity.

1.2–1.4 kg each · 9 pcs / carton · Styrofoam net & heat shrink
ii.

Easy-opened Button Coconut

Branded button-tab built into the top — no machete, no fuss. Pulls open in seconds with a clean drinking aperture. Retail-friendly and bar-friendly.

0.75–1.1 kg each · 9 pcs / carton · Easy-opened button & heat shrink
iii.

Easy-pulled Ring Bald Coconut (Large)

Polished bald sphere with an easy-pull ring sealed into the top. Size Large — for buyers who want a one-coconut single-serve format with maximum yield.

501–650 g each · 24 pcs / carton · Easy-pulled ring & heat shrink
iv.

Hat-shaped Trimmed Coconut

Husk trimmed back to a wide flat base with a peaked top — sits steady on a tray or shelf without rolling. Popular with European retail.

1.0–1.4 kg each · 9 pcs / carton · Styrofoam net & heat shrink
v.

Polished-Bald Coconut (Small)

Husk fully removed, surface polished smooth to a clean white finish. Smallest size band — ideal for tasting menus, in-flight catering, and premium retail packs.

450–600 g each · 32 pcs / carton · Styrofoam net & heat shrink
vi.

Vacuum-Sealed Diamond-Cut Coconut

Our flagship Diamond Cut with the added security of a vacuum seal. Extends shelf life and protects against in-transit pressure changes for long-haul ocean freight.

0.8–1.2 kg each · 9 pcs / carton · Styrofoam net, heat shrink & vacuum-sealing
vii.

Diamond-Cut Coconut

The national-standard export form — clean, faceted, recognisable. Heat-shrunk and labelled, ready to land on shelf with no further handling.

0.8–1.2 kg each · 24 pcs / carton · Styrofoam net & heat shrink
viii.

Polished-Half Bald Coconut (Medium)

Polished bald sphere in the medium size band — the volume workhorse. Best price-to-yield ratio across our range.

601–900 g each · 24 pcs / carton · Styrofoam net & heat shrink
ix.

Laser-engraved Diamond Coconut

Diamond Cut with your logo, QR code, or design laser-engraved directly into the shell — no sticker, no fade. Premium-channel and gifting ready.

0.8–1.2 kg each · 9 pcs / carton · Styrofoam net, heat shrink & customised engraving
